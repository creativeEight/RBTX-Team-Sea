Meet Our Stakeholder

Final Year Students in USM Aquatic Biology (2021/2022)

  • Found that sea water for the Pulau Ketam is around pH 6.8 - pH 7.5

    Optimum pH value of the sea is pH 7.0 - 8.0

  • Undergoes a research based on the increasing of pH value of the sea affects the marine life
  • Found that the exoskeleton marine life has affected the most
  • Interview 30 fishermen and get the result dropping of exoskeleton marine life by 20-30%
  • Add akaline solution to the experimental sea can slow down the ocean acidicification process

    Suggested Method: Electrolysis Method

Theories and Concept

  • SEA reduces the concentration of carbon dioxide using the electrolysis method
  • Carbon dioxide reacts with water become bicarbonate ion (Increase pH value)
  • Make the sea water become sodium hydroxide and hydrochloric acid
  • Increase the pH value by creating more sodium hydroxide (Alkaline)

Proof of Concept

Ocean carbon dioxide removal (CDR) via electrochemistry

CO2 Extraction from seawater using bipolar membrane electro dialysis
